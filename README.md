# The Tool Money Lab Editorial Methodology

> Building trust through transparency.

Welcome to the official editorial methodology repository for **The Tool Money Lab (TTML)**.

This repository documents the principles, standards and review methodology that guide every article, software review, comparison and recommendation published on The Tool Money Lab.

Our objective is simple:

**Help professionals make better software decisions through transparent, independent and evidence-based editorial practices.**

---

## Why this repository exists

Trust is earned through consistency and transparency.

Rather than keeping our editorial standards hidden, we publish them publicly so readers, software vendors and search engines can understand exactly how our content is produced.

This repository serves as the canonical reference for our editorial policies.

---

# Our Core Principles

## Independence

Editorial decisions are made independently.

Commercial partnerships never determine rankings, review scores or recommendations.

---

## Accuracy

Every article is reviewed for factual accuracy before publication.

Where appropriate we verify:

- Features
- Pricing
- Security claims
- Integrations
- Company information
- Product updates

---

## Hands-on Evaluation

Whenever possible we use software ourselves.

Reviews are based on practical experience, product documentation and publicly available information.

---

## Transparency

Affiliate relationships are always disclosed.

Readers should always understand when a commission may be earned.

Transparency builds trust.

---

# Editorial Standards

Every review follows a consistent framework.

This includes:

- Overview
- Key Features
- Pricing
- Pros
- Cons
- Best For
- Alternatives
- Verdict
- Editorial Score

Consistency makes products easier to compare.

---

# Review Methodology

Products are evaluated using multiple criteria including:

- Ease of Use
- Feature Set
- Performance
- Value for Money
- Customer Support
- Documentation
- Security
- Innovation
- Reliability

No single criterion determines the final score.

---

# Artificial Intelligence Policy

AI assists our editorial workflow.

AI may help with:

- Research
- Draft preparation
- Grammar
- Formatting
- Data organisation

Every published article receives human editorial review before publication.

Editorial judgement always remains human.

---

# Affiliate Policy

The Tool Money Lab participates in selected affiliate programmes.

Affiliate commissions never influence:

- Rankings
- Scores
- Recommendations
- Editorial conclusions

Products cannot purchase favourable reviews.

---

# Corrections Policy

Technology changes quickly.

If we discover outdated or inaccurate information we update our content as quickly as possible.

We welcome correction requests from readers and software vendors.

---

# Versioning

Our methodology continues to evolve as technology changes.

Major updates will be documented within this repository.
